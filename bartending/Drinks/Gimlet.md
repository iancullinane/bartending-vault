## Vodka

2oz [[Vodka]]
1oz [[lime juice]]
1oz [[lemon juice]]

[[Shake]] and pour over rocks in a [[highball glass]], top with [[tonic water]].

Add more club soda if need to make is N/A.

### Or with lime cordial

Replace [[lime juice]] with [[Lime cordial]].
